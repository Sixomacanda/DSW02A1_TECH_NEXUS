About Us


Urban Track is a smart platform designed to help communities report and manage public issues quickly and efficiently. Our mission is to empower citizens to take an active role in maintaining their cities.



Founded in 2026, Urban Track connects residents with local authorities, ensuring that problems like infrastructure issues, safety concerns, and environmental hazards are addressed promptly.



Join thousands of users who are already making a difference in their communities. Together, we can build better cities for everyone.
