About UrbanTrack

UrbanTrack is a smart community issue reporting platform that gives residents a simple, central place to report local problems like potholes, broken streetlights, blocked drains and vandalism. Instead of calls going unanswered and emails getting lost, every report is logged, mapped and visible to the whole community. Neighbours can upvote issues they recognise, pushing the most urgent problems to the top so local authorities know exactly where to focus first. Admins manage incoming reports, update statuses in real time, and keep residents informed every step of the way from submission right through to resolution. UrbanTrack brings transparency, accountability and community power together in one place, making it easier for people to be heard and faster for problems to get fixed.
